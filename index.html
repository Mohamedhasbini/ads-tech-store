Policies

Straightforward terms for buying, delivery, and after-sales. Questions? Message us on WhatsApp →

Shipping & Delivery

  • Delivery across Lebanon. Beirut and suburbs typically arrive in 1–2 business days; other regions 2–4 business days.
  • Orders are confirmed on WhatsApp before dispatch — we verify stock, final price, and your delivery address.
  • A delivery fee may apply depending on your area; it is always quoted upfront, never a surprise at the door.
  • Pickup from our Hamra shop (Makdessi Street) is available during opening hours (Mon–Sat, 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM).

Returns & Warranty

  • All products are 100% genuine and carry the manufacturer's warranty.
  • Dead-on-arrival items: contact us within 48 hours of delivery and we'll arrange a replacement or repair.
  • Warranty claims are handled through our repair lab — bring the device or send it with us and we coordinate with the manufacturer.
  • Software issues, accidental damage, and normal wear are not covered by warranty, but our repair team can always help for a quote.

Privacy

  • We only collect the details needed to fulfil your order — name, phone/WhatsApp, and delivery address.
  • We never sell or share your information with third parties.
  • Order conversations happen on WhatsApp and are subject to WhatsApp's own privacy terms.

Terms

  • Prices are shown in USD. Payment in LBP is accepted at the day's exchange rate, confirmed on WhatsApp.
  • Availability is subject to stock; if an item sells out we'll offer the closest alternative.
  • Payment is cash on delivery, plus Whish and OMT where available.
  • Specifications and images are provided by manufacturers and may vary slightly by batch.
